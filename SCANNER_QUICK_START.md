## ðŸ¦  Common Diseases Detected

1. **Leaf Spot**
   - Brown/black spots on leaves
   - Fungal infection
   - Treat with fungicide

2. **Powdery Mildew**
   - White powdery coating
   - Fungal disease
   - Improve air circulation

3. **Rust**
   - Orange/rust colored pustules
   - Fungal infection
   - Remove infected parts

4. **Blight**
   - Rapid wilting/browning
   - Very serious
   - Immediate treatment needed

5. **Yellowing/Chlorosis**
   - Leaf yellowing
   - Nutrient deficiency
   - Check fertilization

6. **Pest Damage**
   - Holes in leaves
   - Insect activity
   - Use appropriate pesticide
